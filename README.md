## StudyMate

A fullstack web application.

> Programming hero assignment 10.

This website helps you find a study partner who can join you during your study sessions and make your learning more effective.

**Visit live site:** [StudyMate](https://studymate-assignment-10.vercel.app/)

##### Key features

- **Secure Accounts** – Your privacy and data are always protected.
- **Cloud-Synced Data** – Never lose your study info or saved partners.
- **Smart Partner Search** – Find study partners using advanced filters.
- **Easy Connections** – Add or remove study partners with a single click.
- **Dark & Light Themes** – Switch anytime for comfortable studying.
